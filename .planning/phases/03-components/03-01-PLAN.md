3. Ease-out cubic: `1 - Math.pow(1 - progress, 3)` — decelerates naturally.

9. Color tokens from globals.css: `bg-danger` (Traditional), `bg-warning` (EU line), `bg-success` (ECO STAR), `bg-primary` (NATURA).
